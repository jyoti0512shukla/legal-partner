ARTICLE 2 — SERVICES

4.1. Scope of Services.
Licensor shall provide Licensee with a perpetual, non-exclusive, non-transferable, and non-sublicensable license to use the NovaTech Clinical Suite v4.0 software in object code format, limited to 500 named user licenses across three hospital locations. Licensor shall provide Licensee with the source code escrow required in sub-clause 4.2. Licensor shall provide Licensee with regular security patch updates as described in sub-clause 4.3.

4.2. Change Request Procedure.
Licensor and Licensee shall agree on a change request procedure for any requested changes to the NovaTech Clinical Suite v4.0 software. This procedure shall be documented in a separate document, which shall be attached as Schedule B to this Agreement.

4.3. Service Standards and Acceptance Criteria.
Licensor shall provide Licensee with 24/7 critical bug support subject to a 4-hour response SLA and shall provide Licensee with quarterly security patch updates. Licensee shall have the right to create derivative works for internal use only, subject to certain restrictions set forth in Section 5 of this Agreement.

Support Response Levels: (a) Severity 1 (Critical — system down or data at risk): Response within 4 hours, 24/7 coverage. (b) Severity 2 (Major — significant functionality impaired): Response within 8 business hours. (c) Severity 3 (Minor — non-critical issues): Response within 2 business days. Service credits shall apply for failure to meet Severity 1 response times as set forth in Schedule B.
