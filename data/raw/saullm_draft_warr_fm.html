3. EFFECT: Affected obligations are suspended for the duration of the Force Majeure Event. Neither Party is in breach for suspended obligations. The affected Party must use reasonable endeavours to perform its obligations despite the event to the extent possible.

4. MITIGATION: The affected Party shall take all reasonable steps to minimise the impact of the Force Majeure Event and resume full performance as soon as reasonably practicable. It must provide fortnightly updates on the duration and expected cessation of the event.
